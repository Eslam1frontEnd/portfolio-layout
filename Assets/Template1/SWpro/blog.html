Digital transformation and digital banking.

Given the trends of digital transformation and changing customer expectations, banks have had to transform their business to remain relevant to their customers. The new competition from financial technology, digital banks, and mobile banks has posed new challenges for traditional banks.

deposits and how to make use of them.

When you own an amount of money, you go to the bank and deposit it as a time deposit, and the term is a certain period, which may be a year or several consecutive years; That is, you are not allowed to withdraw that deposit until after the term has expired, at which time the bank will refund the full amount to you in addition to the contracted interest.

Visa card made it faster and easier.

The visa card is a plastic and electronic card that is linked with bank accounts, and helps to use the money available in the account in return for deducting a small amount of money, in return for obtaining this banking service; Therefore, the Visa card is considered one of the things that cannot be dispensed with in banks, which is used in many purchases and sales..

Loans and their impact on your life.

If you need to start your own business and you do not have enough money, you can take a loan from the bank with a very simple monthly or annual return, and specify a repayment period of up to five years, and until the amount is paid, you will have to work to provide this borrowing amount to pay it to the bank because you take this loan with guarantees.
